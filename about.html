About Auryce

Auryce is an international senior advisory house providing services to clients, in the natural resource and commodities sectors, relating to investment, partnership/alliance development, project study, mergers and acquisitions, divestitures, financings and commercial. With our passionate and pragmatic team oriented toward sustainable value creation, Auryce brings together experts with decades of international experiences with major companies whilst operating in emerging markets as well as in developed economies including the Middle East, Africa, China, America and Europe.
